Fixed-Fee Discovery Engagement

03 :: SCOPE

A focused, high-value engagement designed to produce a concrete, actionable blueprint for your AI journey.

Stakeholder Workshops

Independent Analysis

  • Review provided documentation and system diagrams.

  • Develop AI Opportunity Matrix and draft final report.
PHASE_03

Final Presentation

  • Executive presentation of the Assessment Report.
  • Review of Opportunity Matrix.
